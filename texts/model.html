• Normalization: Imagery bands percentile-clipped [1–99%] and scaled to [0,1]; Meteorology z-scored & labels log-transformed and z-scored.
  • Stage 1 — Backbone Pretraining: Train all components (ResNet50, S5P-CNN, Meteo-MLP) on a balanced dataset augmented with synthetic rural stations (countering urban station bias for better global generalization).
  • Stage 2 — Head Fine-tuning: Freeze backbones; train only pollutant-specific heads on real-world data. Masked loss enables learning from partial labels (backpropagate only when target available).
